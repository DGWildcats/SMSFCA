Who are we?

We are a group of young Christian students devoted to sharing the good grace of God with everyone who wants to hear it! We meet before schools on Wednesdays @ 7am. The requirements are very minimal. All you need to do is show up! (You don't have to be Christian or Athletic) Click over to the about us tab and you will find more information.
