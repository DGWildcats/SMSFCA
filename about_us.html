About Us

What is the point?


Some of you may ask yourselves...what is FCA? FCA stands for Fellowship of Christian Athletes. This is a club from the orginial orginization. Here is the website. FCA is a place where young Christians have the opprutunity to gather in a public school and live out their faith. We may play a game or we'll do a lesson. We have events where we all just hangout after school like for example we sometimes go to an indoor trampoline park or go see a movie. Over all this is a club where students have an opprutunity to share their testimony to those that need it or just need a refresh from the week of school. We meet @ 7am on Wednesdays. Hope we will see you there. If you want to see the clubs leaders, go ahead and click over to the leaders tab.
